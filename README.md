# policy-planning

**Full policy planning pipeline from research to submission — built from 6 real government and campaign projects.**

## Goal

Policy work demands rigor across research, stakeholder mapping, legal constraints, and political strategy. Policy-Planning streamlines the entire pipeline using real patterns from government and campaign projects. It integrates campaign strategy templates (8 distinct plans) for different policy contexts.

## When & How to Use

Use this skill when initiating policy research, drafting a policy proposal, or planning a policy campaign. The skill runs a complete pipeline: research-frame integration for multi-axis investigation → axis research → spine extraction for core argument → proposal drafting → submission cleanup. Input policy question → submission-ready proposal.

## Use Cases

| Scenario | Prompt | What Happens |
|---|---|---|
| Environmental regulation | `"policy-planning: draft proposal for carbon pricing"` | Research precedents→stakeholder mapping→spine extraction→proposal draft→submission prep |
| Agency rulemaking | `"Policy-planning: data privacy rule for healthcare IoT"` | Research legal/technical→axis analysis→core argument→formal rule proposal |
| Ballot measure strategy | `"Campaign plan for housing zoning reform"` | Campaign template: research→messaging→stakeholder coalition→approval pathway |

## Key Features

- Complete pipeline: research-frame → stakeholder/impact/precedent axes → spine extraction → proposal drafting → submission cleanup
- 8 campaign strategy templates for different policy contexts
- Built on patterns from 6 real government and campaign projects
- Multi-stakeholder impact analysis integrated
- Legal and political constraint mapping
